Getting Started

Our programs are developed and delivered by longtime academics, centered around getting the best results for you, the student. We have proven programs of study that are individualized to your specifics. Our success is tied to your success, so we will all do well together!

The process is easy, too! After an initial intake we will schedule times where we link up over a clear Voice/Video over IP connection. We may also employ a whiteboard app for some instruction. We will work through your personalized curriculum that was created during your intake, and built around your specific needs. Each session lasts approximately one hour, usually with some homework to get done before the next meeting. After the program is complete, each student receives a written narrative outlining progress made toward objectives determined during the intake. Feel free to contact us if you have more questions or would like to set up an intake session. If you have already decided on a package, you may choose an option below.
